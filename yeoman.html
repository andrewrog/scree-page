Yeoman Snow Report

Went skiing today in the morning up at Yeoman Campground with mom. It was quite warm weather, it almost felt like skiing in the spring time. The snow density and the air temperature were very spring-like. There was one spot on the trail up where we had to take our skis off and walk. There was just not enough coverage in this spot, given the large amount of sunlight that it receives. The main road and north facing slopes are still holding snow quite well. Overall a great ski. I encourage others to get up there when it's sunny because it is very beautiful and scenic up there. The drive is worth it!

Expert balance required ;|
