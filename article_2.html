So, this is actually a nice straight forward swap (unlike a lot of swaps out there). There are not many wiring hurdles that need to be overcome to get this to work. That is if you get the right engine.

Got the engine. It has an unknown number of miles, at least that is what the scrap yard I got it from said. So that is definitely a little worrisome. This EJ22 does have new cylinder heads though. These heads have hydraulic lifters, instead of solid style lifters. They are also single port exhaust, which means I had to pick up a new exhaust manifold. The previous engine in the car had dual port exhaust cylinder heads. Below is a picture of the engine (I worked on cleaning it up a little but it is still pretty dirty).
